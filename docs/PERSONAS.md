# Stakeholder Personas for Qualification Service

The **Qualification** microservice is the final gatekeeper of the ecosystem. It manages the Bid/No-Bid workflow.

## 1. [Sascha, the Decision Maker](../../docs/product/personas/sascha/persona.md)
**Role:** Public Sector Expert & Chief Bid Qualifier
**Relationship to Qualification:**
- **Responsibility:** Making the final decision on whether to invest resources in a bid.
- **Needs:** A consolidated view of AI summaries, scores, and historical win/loss benchmarks.
- **Pain Point:** Information overload when multiple high-rated tenders arrive simultaneously.

## 2. [Björn, the Ressort Lead](../../docs/product/personas/bjoern/persona.md)
**Role:** Executive Portfolio Manager
**Relationship to Qualification:**
- **Responsibility:** Approving high-value bids (>€500k) that require significant upfront investment.
- **Needs:** Visibility into the "Qualification Pipeline" to forecast ressort utilization and revenue.
- **Pain Point:** Lack of early warning for massive tenders that might strain cluster capacity.

## 3. [Daniel, the Manager](../../docs/product/personas/daniel/persona.md)
**Role:** Delivery Manager
**Relationship to Qualification:**
- **Relationship:** Daniel is often assigned as the "Bid Owner" once a tender is qualified.
- **Needs:** Clear handover documentation and the rationale behind the "Go" decision.
- **Pain Point:** Inheriting a qualified bid that turns out to be a poor fit due to unrecorded risks.

## 4. [Ambika, the Agile Coach](../../docs/product/personas/ambika/persona.md)
**Role:** First-time Bid Driver
**Relationship to Qualification:**
- **Responsibility:** Executing the bid process once qualified.
- **Needs:** A structured, guided workflow for the qualification steps to ensure nothing is missed.
- **Pain Point:** Feeling overwhelmed by the complexity of the internal bid approval process.
