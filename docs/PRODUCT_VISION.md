# Product Vision: Qualification Microservice

> **The Definitive Gate for Bid Selection Excellence**

## Vision Statement
To provide a seamless, collaborative, and evidence-based workflow for Bid/No-Bid decisions, ensuring that every "Go" decision is backed by quantified data and expert consensus.

## The Strategy
| Aspect | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Target Group** | Bid Managers (Sascha), Ressort Leads (Björn), and Delivery Managers (Daniel). |
| **Needs** | Decision workflow management, risk assessment tools, and executive approval gates. |
| **Product** | A workflow-centric microservice that synthesizes scores and human review into a definitive qualification status. |
| **Business Goals** | Reduce "Go" decision time by 50%, increase the quality of qualified bids, and ensure 100% auditability. |

## Key Features
1.  **Guided Qualification Workflow**: Step-by-step process for evaluating tenders against the "Definition of Ready".
2.  **Risk & Opportunity Matrix**: Interactive tool for assessing project risks and business potential.
3.  **Collaborative Review**: Multi-persona feedback loop (Manager, Architect, Exec) for complex bids.
4.  **Audit Trail**: Permanent record of who made the final decision and why.

## Success Metrics
- **Decision Speed**: Average time from "Enriched" to "Qualified" status.
- **Approval Integrity**: % of bids that pass executive review on the first attempt.
- **Outcome Tracking**: Correlation between the qualification rationale and actual project success.
